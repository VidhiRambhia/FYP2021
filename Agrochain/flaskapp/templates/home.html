Our model

01

Farmers

They are the heart of our system. Farmers harvest the crops and register the crop with its quantity on our system.

02

Buying centres

Buying centres buy the entire produce from multiple local farmers, serialise the batches and sell them to hubs for storage.

03

Hubs

Hubs are conditioned storage facilities where produce is graded, sorted and packaged. They are essentially the suppliers that sell packaged products to retailers.

04

Retailers

Retailers buy products from nearest hubs and sell them off to customers.

05

Customers

Hey Customer! Feel free to go through our system and trace the product you just bought to check its authenticity.
